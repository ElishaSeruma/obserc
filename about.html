About Obserc

Transforming personal safety with AI-powered technology, Obserc empowers individuals and communities to live without fear.

Learn More

Our Mission

Violence, especially in domestic and personal settings, is a silent crisis affecting millions worldwide. People often suffer in silence — feeling scared, isolated, and unsure where to turn.

At Obserc, our mission is to break that silence. We create AI-powered safety technology that detects emotional, verbal, and physical violence early — helping people get support before situations escalate.

We believe everyone deserves to feel safe and supported in their daily lives, no matter where they are or what they’re doing.

What We Do

Obserc uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze biometric signals — like voice tone, facial expressions, and behavior — to recognize signs of distress and danger in real time.

Unlike traditional emergency systems, Obserc quietly works in the background, protecting your privacy while constantly learning and adapting to your unique patterns.

When Obserc detects a possible threat, it instantly alerts your trusted Circle — a group of friends, family, and emergency contacts you choose. If your Circle can’t respond, Obserc escalates the situation safely by connecting you with helplines or emergency services.

This layered approach balances immediate human support with AI-guided escalation, creating a responsive safety net tailored for real-world situations — from late-night walks to remote work, first dates, or moments when you simply feel uneasy.

Why It Matters

Violence is complex and often hidden behind layers of fear and stigma. Early intervention is critical — it can prevent harm, reduce trauma, and empower survivors to seek help sooner.

Obserc’s unique combination of AI-driven detection and trusted human networks aims to shift the balance of power back to individuals. By providing timely alerts and context-aware responses, we help people reclaim control over their safety and well-being.

Our technology is designed with deep respect for user privacy, consent, and autonomy — because true safety means feeling secure in both your environment and your data.

Our Vision

We envision a future where safety is built into everyday life — where technology serves as a quiet guardian, empowering people to live without fear.

Obserc is more than an app; it’s a commitment to transforming how we think about personal security, community support, and the role of AI in human lives.

Our goal is to build safer, more connected communities worldwide by combining the power of artificial intelligence with the strength of human relationships.

Join Us

Obserc is a collective effort. Whether you’re a user looking for peace of mind, a partner building safer ecosystems, or an advocate fighting for survivors’ rights — your voice matters.

Together, we can create a future where fear no longer controls lives, where technology amplifies compassion, and where everyone has a trusted safety companion by their side.

Join us on this journey. Let’s build safety, support, and empowerment — one connection at a time.
