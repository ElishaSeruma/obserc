Obserc Beta Phases & Roadmap

Our beta program is designed to iterate quickly but deliberately. Below is the breakdown of each phase and what it focuses on, guiding us toward a robust personal safety companion.

Phase 1: Headless Detection Completed

Goal: Validate AI detection accuracy without UI complexity.

Key Tests:

  • Emotional, verbal, and physical violence detection rates.

Outcome: Achieved 88% detection accuracy in varied real-world environments.

Completion Date: July 2025

View Headless Builds

Phase 2: UI Preview In Progress

Goal: Test user interaction flows, Circle features, and manual escalation tools.

Current Focus:

  • Streamlining navigation for intuitive use.
  • Refining feedback animations for real-time alerts.

Estimated Completion: September 2025

Try UI Preview Builds

Phase 3: Full UI & Analytics Planned

Goal: Integrate full design language, personal safety analytics, and onboarding flow.

Extras:

  • Data visualization for safety trends.
  • AI confidence indicators for detection reliability.

Target: Late 2025

Phase 4: Public Release Candidate Planned

Goal: Ensure stability, scalability, and pre-launch marketing readiness.

Target: Early 2026

Upcoming Milestones

  • Private tester summit for UI review – September 2025
  • Closed-group trial for analytics features – November 2025


Transforming personal safety with AI-powered technology, Obserc empowers individuals and communities to live without fear.
