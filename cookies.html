Obserc — Cookie Policy

Last updated: August 8, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Obserc uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and Beta Services. We aim to be transparent about how we collect and use data to enhance your experience while respecting your privacy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website or use our Beta Services. They help us improve functionality, analyze usage, and provide personalized experiences. We may also use similar technologies like pixels or local storage.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as maintaining your session or enabling core features of the Beta Services.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our website and Beta Services, collecting anonymized data on page views, clicks, and usage patterns.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced features, such as remembering your preferences or settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant content or ads, if applicable, based on your interests.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used to:

  • Ensure the website and Beta Services operate smoothly.
  • Analyze site performance and user behavior to improve our services.
  • Personalize your experience, such as remembering your language preferences.
  • Track engagement with Beta Program features to refine functionality.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Cookie Consent Banner: When visiting our website, you can manage cookie preferences via our consent banner, where applicable.
  • Beta Services Settings: Within the Beta app, you may have options to toggle non-essential data collection features.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g., analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These are subject to the third parties’ privacy policies, and we ensure they comply with data protection standards.

Data Protection and Cookies

Cookie data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy. We use encryption and other security measures to protect cookie-related data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our website and Beta Services evolve. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
