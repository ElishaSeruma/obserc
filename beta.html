Shaping the Future of Safety — Together

The Obserc Beta Program gives you early access to the most advanced AI-powered safety companion — so you can help build the tool you want by your side.

How the Beta Works

The Obserc Beta Program is your chance to explore upcoming features before anyone else — and to shape how they evolve. We roll out in phases, each with a unique Release Code that signals the stage and focus of development.

  • Controlled Access: Every tester receives a personal Auth Code to unlock downloads.
  • Feature-Specific Phases: From core backend intelligence to UI and hardware integration, each phase is focused.
  • Your Feedback Drives Change: What you report directly influences what we build next.
